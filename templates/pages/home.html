Who am I?

My name is Christopher Garcia. At the moment, I am 22 years old. Currently, I am studying Bio Informatics at Hogeschool Leiden. I have experience in Full Stack Web Development, but i enjoy backend development more. In addition to my studies, I like to keep improving my programming skills by working on personal projects and exploring new technologies. I am especially interested in how software can be used in scientific research and data analysis, and I hope to further develop my expertise in this area in the future.

This study interests me because I get to explore and understand what I see as God's creation in a deeper, more structured way. I enjoy learning about the complexity and beauty of living systems, and studying them from a scientific perspective helps me appreciate how everything is connected and functions on different levels. Besides this, I also have a strong passion for programming. I enjoy solving problems, building applications, and turning ideas into working software. Programming gives me a creative outlet as well as a logical challenge, which makes it a great balance alongside my studies in bioinformatics. Combining these two interests allows me to work at the intersection of biology and technology, which I find both meaningful and motivating.
